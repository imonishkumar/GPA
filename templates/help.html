Sign Up

Welcome to our platform! Follow these steps to create your account:

Step 1: Getting Started

To create a new account and start using our platform:

  • Visit our landing page by going to our website or app.
Step 2: Providing Your Information

Once you're on the landing page:

  • Fill out the sign-up form with the following information:
    • Username: Choose a unique username for your account.
    • Email: Provide a valid email address that you have access to.
    • Security Image: Choose any image to upload for added security.
Step 3: Moving Forward

After filling out the form, click the "Next" button. This will take you to the next step in the sign-up process.

Step 4: Verifying Your Security Image

You'll now see the image you uploaded for security. Your task here is to select three specific points on this image, following a particular order. Make sure to remember the order in which you choose these points.

Step 5: Selecting Points

Click on the image at three different spots according to the order given. The system will display the coordinates of these points.

Step 6: Completing Your Account Setup

Once you've successfully selected three points, click the "Create" button. This action finalizes the creation of your account.

Step 7: Confirmation

Congratulations! You'll receive a confirmation message indicating that your account has been successfully created.

Step 8: Access Your Account

You can now log in using your newly created account. Simply enter your username and password to access all the features and services on our platform.

Login

Welcome back! Follow these steps to log in to your account:

Step 1: Access the Login Page

To access your account on our platform, open your preferred web browser and enter the URL of our application into the address bar.

Step 2: Enter Your Email

Upon reaching the login page, you will encounter a login form, including a field labeled "Email."

Input Your Registered Email: In this field, input the email address that you have previously associated with your account.

Step 3: Click "Next"

After entering your email, proceed by clicking the "Next" button to initiate the login process.

Step 4: Authenticate Using an Image

You will be redirected to a page featuring an image as part of our enhanced security measures.

Select Three Distinct Points: To complete the authentication process, it is imperative that you select three specific points on the displayed image.

Point Selection: Carefully click on the image at the designated locations as specified in the instructions. It is crucial to remember the order in which you select these points.

Step 5: Submit Authentication

Following the selection of three points on the image, proceed by clicking the "Submit" button.

Step 6: Access Your Account

If you accurately selected the points within the defined criteria, our system will seamlessly redirect you to your personalized account's homepage.

Access Granted: Congratulations! You have successfully logged into your account and are now granted access to the comprehensive array of features and resources that our platform offers.

Troubleshooting

Incorrect Email: If, by any chance, you input an incorrect email address, our system will promptly notify you with an error message indicating a mismatch. In such cases, kindly verify the accuracy of the entered email. If you are new to our platform, you also have the option to initiate the account creation process.

Forgot Password

Forgot your password? No worries. Follow these steps to reset your password:

Step 1: Click on "Forgot Password" Link

If you've forgotten your password, don't worry. Follow these steps to reset it:

Start by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

Step 2: Provide Your Email Address

You'll be redirected to the "Password Reset Request" page.

Enter your registered email address into the provided field.

Click the "SUBMIT" button to proceed.

If your email exists in our system, you'll receive an email with instructions on how to reset your password.

Don't forget to check your inbox and spam folder for this email.

Step 3: Open the Password Reset Email

Check your registered email inbox for a message titled "Password Reset Request."

Open the email to proceed with the password reset process.

Step 4: Click on "Reset Password" Link

Inside the email, you'll find a link that says "Reset Password." Click on this link to continue.

The link will take you to a secure page for resetting your password.

Step 5: Upload a Security Image

On the reset password page, you will be prompted to upload a security image.

Click on the "Choose File" button to select an image file from your device.

Ensure that the image you upload is in an accepted format (e.g., JPEG, PNG).

After selecting the image, proceed by clicking the "NEXT" button.

Step 6: Select Three Points on the Image

Next, you will see the uploaded security image.

You must select three specific points on the image in a particular order.

Click on the image at the desired locations as instructed. Remember the sequence in which you select these points.

The system will automatically record your selected points.

Step 7: Submit Authentication

After selecting three points on the image, click the "CREATE" button.

If you've correctly selected the points in the specified order, your password will be successfully reset.

Step 8: Access Your Account

Upon successful password reset, you will be redirected to the login page.

Use your newly reset password to log in to your account.

You can now access all the features and services on our platform with your updated credentials.
